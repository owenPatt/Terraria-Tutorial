Progression

After Hardmode

Right after defeating the Wall of Flesh

After the Wall of Flesh is defeated, all enemies will become much stronger. New enemies, that of course are much harder to kill, start to appear as well.

The Wall of Flesh should have dropped the Pwnhammer. You use this hammer to destroy Demon Altars, which spawns hardmode ores into your world. This is the main way to progress after hardmode is entered.

After Mining the Hardmode Ores

After you are geared with the tier three hardmode ore that spawned and whatever class specific items you can make at this point, it is time to defeat the mechanical bosses. These bosses are:

  1. The Destroyer
  2. The Twins
  3. The Skeletron Prime

They should also be fought in the order of 1, 2 and then 3. This will be the easiest way to defeat them.

Post Mechanical bosses

After the Mechanical Bosses, you will want to start getting ready for the fight against Plantera. Plantera is located within the jungle, and once killed two paths will open. First, you will gain access to the updated dungeon. The dungeon will begin to spawn much hard enemies that drop extremely good loot, which will most likely have to be grinded for. This is more of a side quest, as you do not need to do it for the main progression, however, I would recommend spending some time to gather some of the looted located within as they are very powerful. The other path Plantera opens is the jungle temple. This temple houses the next main boss, Golem.
