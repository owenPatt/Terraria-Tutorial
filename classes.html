Classes

What are the classes in Terraria?

Choosing a class in Terraria changes a lot about how the player is going to interact in the world. Some of the differences that will be experienced are: There are different items and accessories that need to be collected with each classes, each boss fight will feel much different depending on the class, and ,most importantly, how the player's team will synergize depends on the classes they choose. Since Terraria is a Multiplayer game, the overall teamwork should be looked at when chooses which classes to play as. Once a class is chosen, you are not stuck with that class. Changing armor and gear changes what classes the player is. However, a lot of the class specific gear can be very hard and timely to get. This is why, even though classes can be changed freely, it is recommended that you choose and stick with a class.
