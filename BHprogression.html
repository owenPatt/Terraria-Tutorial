Progression

Before Hardmode

Day

01

When making your world, make sure you choose the size good with the amount of players you have. Also, I would recommend choosing corruption as the evil biome if you are a new player. This is much easier than the Crimson, the other evil biome. Once you start, a good idea is to create shelter. First chop down some trees and create a house for you and the Guide, the starting villager.

Day

02

Start exploring the map. Make sure you have the surface descovered, and then start underground. There are many items and trinkets that are biome specific, so make sure to explore some of all the major biomes.

Day

30

By this time you should have reached the max amount of Health for this stage in the game, 200 HP. This is achieved through collecting and using heart crystals. After you hit 200 health, your first boss will have a chance to spawn at night, the Eye of Cthulhu. Make sure to create a small arena before hitting 200 health. After defeating the Eye of Cthulhu, you will be ready to fight the next boss.

Day

40

After you explored the surface of your world at the beginning of the game, you should have found the evil biome in your game. This biome will either be the Crimson or Corruption. Depending on the biome, the next boss will be different. How to spawn the boss will be the same though. Inside of the evil biome, there will be orbs underground in the main cave area. You must blow these up with TNT or dynamite. Create the armor and weapons that you can after defeating the boss. This will be what you use in the next fight.

Day

45

After you explored the surface of your world at the beginning of the game, you should have found the dungeon. The dungeon is cursed and will not allow anyone to enter until you defeat Skeletron. At this point in the game, you should be able to defeat Skeletron. Skeletron can be very hard to defeat, make sure you prepare by making potions and creating a large arena near the dungeon you can fight him in. After, explore the dungeon.

Day

60

After fully exploring the dungeon and creating weapons and better gear from this, you will now venture into the underworld. This is at the very bottom of the world. You can collect hellstone from the underworld that makes better armor. After creating the armor, you will be ready to defeat the Wall of Flesh. Once you are ready, drop a guide voodoo doll into the lava in the underworld, killing the guild and spawning the Wall of Flesh.

Timeline

This timeline provided is based on days in Terraria for Pre-Hardmode. Hardmode is entered once you defeat the wall of flesh, and everything before the wall of flesh is considered "early game". Of course you do not need to follow the exact days on the timeline, however, the general ideas should be followed if you want the most success progressing through the early game, before hardmode.
